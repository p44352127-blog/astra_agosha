What inspired the "Secrets of Life" robots? +
The project was inspired by observing how small insects like bees and ants manage complex agricultural tasks through simple, repetitive movements.
Currently, they are semi-autonomous. They use Arduino Nano sensors to navigate but follow a pre-programmed logic for specific farming tasks.
Full schematics and the ISEF project board layout are available upon request through the Contact page.
